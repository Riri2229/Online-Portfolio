About Me

I am a sophomore student at Dublin High School, where I have been enrolled for the past two years. This year, I embarked on my journey into Computer Science Education (CSE). I aspire to further my education at institutions such as the University of Southern California (USC), the University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB), or the Berklee School of Music in Michigan. While my passion lies in music, I am also keen on integrating coding into music production. My goal is to excel in coding and utilize it to enhance my music production capabilities.

I am currently volunteering for my dance classes. Volunteering has provided me with a new perspective on not only how to collaborate with others but also how to lead a group. It has enabled me to discover new ways of thinking and navigate through challenging obstacles. This experience has contributed significantly to enhancing my confidence and improving my communication skills.
